A system to prescribe the medicine for given symptoms

Abstract: There is a famous proverb in Hindi: “Pehla Sukh Nirogi Kaya” “Prescribe Medicines for the Given Symptoms” is a UG project to provide the medical assistance for the users. Present time is the era of very fast delivery of information with effectiveness and accuracy. This is the time of smartphones, tablets and smart computers which are connected to each other and information travels with the speed of light. Better human health is the first priority of life. So why should not use the technology for the upgrading the health level of human life. Our project deals with the use of present technological aspects in the field of Computer Science to make a system to provide the medical assistance for the user with a single click on electronically devices.

Motivation

The main objective of a software is to solve the real life human problems and make the life better. From a long time bad human health is the main problem of human life. So that's why we chosen this project to make a software for the medical assistance which is based on the current technological scenarios.
The project deals with the present time health problems and can be useful to everyone.
The validity of the project is for a very long time period.
Validity and scope of the project facilitate many developers to work and implement new ideas in the project.
Non-technical people also contribute in the projects.
Project required much and much survey, reports and interactions to make it feasible.
The implementation of the project is going to provide knowledge and cover the major part of our studies.
